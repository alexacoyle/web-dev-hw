Banana Scrambled Eggs

Ready In: Serves:
4 minutes 1

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 medium banana - preferably ripe
  • ground cinamon
  • vanilla extract
  • coconut oil
  • nut butter of choice - optional for topping
  • fruit of choice - optional for topping


Directions

  1. Line small frying pan with coconut oil (or use a dab of butter/non-stick spray)

  2. Turn the stove on medium low heat.

  3. In a small bowl, mash the banana (until there are no chunks)

  4. Add the egg to mashed banana - whisk together until combined

  5. Add a few sprinkles of ground cinanmon - amount to your liking

  6. Add dash of vanilla extract

  7. Combine all ingredients

  8. Add mixture to pan, stir slightly to cook (like you would scramble eggs) - DO NOT OVERCOOK! A little undercooked is fine, since the heat will continue to cook the eggs for a while after you remove from pan.

  9. Scoop eggs onto a plate, drizzle with nut butter and fruit (if desired), and bon appetite!

  10. Serve immediately.
