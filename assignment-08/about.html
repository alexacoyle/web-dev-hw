For this project I chose banana scrambled eggs because this is my favorite breakfast recipe, and one that not many people know about. It has made a big impact on my eating habits as I eat very healthy for my sport and athletic performance.


I crave sweet foods in the morning opposed to savory tastes, but want to eliminate high sugar foods from my diet. This recipe provides a perfect sweet breakfast alternative, including all healthy ingredients with nutrients, protein, and no sugar.


You can learn more about this recipe by simply researching it on the internet. It is not widely known but it is on the web.
